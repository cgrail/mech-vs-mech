grails.de Mech vs. Mech Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this app handles information when it is used, including when players use the multiplayer feature.

Overview

This app is designed to minimize data use. It does not require account creation, does not maintain user profiles, and does not collect or store personal data for analytics, marketing, or account management.

Information Handling

The app does not collect personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials.

The app does not create user accounts.

The app does not use analytics, advertising identifiers, or behavior tracking technologies.

The app does not retain gameplay data, multiplayer history, or personal identifiers on a persistent basis.

Multiplayer Sessions

The app includes an optional multiplayer mode that allows players to participate in the same game session without creating an account.

To make multiplayer gameplay work, the app temporarily transmits the minimum technical and in-game session data needed to synchronize the live game state between participating players.

This data is used only while the session is active.

This data is not stored after the session ends.

No persistent player profile, friend list, chat history, or match history is created.

Data Storage

Data used for multiplayer synchronization is processed only transiently for session operation.

No personal data or session data is stored on a long-term basis by the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics or advertising services.

If networking infrastructure such as relay or transport servers is used to enable real-time multiplayer communication, that infrastructure is used only to pass session data needed for live gameplay and not to create persistent user records.

Children’s Privacy

Because the app does not collect or store personal data, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected in the version made available with the app or on the app’s product page.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be directed to the app developer through the contact method provided on the app’s App Store product page.

App Store Privacy Summary

For App Store privacy labeling purposes, this app is intended to operate without collecting data linked to the user and without tracking users across apps or websites. Temporary multiplayer synchronization data is used only to provide app functionality during an active game session and is not stored after the session ends.
